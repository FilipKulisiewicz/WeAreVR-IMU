Detailed Description

Conveys the motion relative to the body frame of the Freespace handheld device. The data have been processed to remove tremor and other unwanted side effects.

◆ angularVelX

int16_t freespace_BodyFrame::angularVelX

Angular Velocity is reported in units of rad/s with an exponent of -3. X positive is tilt right(roll). Y positive it tilt up(pitch). Z positive is turn right(yaw) wrt the handheld device frame of reference.
